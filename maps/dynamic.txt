An 8x8 map designed for testing replanning.
The initial obstacles are static. The "dynamic" aspect is
that the agent should be able to re-route if an unexpected
obstacle were to appear on its path.
Agent starts at S(0,0).
Package at P(1,1) needs to go to G(7,7).
